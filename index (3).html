Welcome to My Portfolio

Assalamu'alaikum and hello! I am a BBA student at IIUM. This portfolio reflects my journey of self-development in the light of Quran and Sunnah, through the USRAH 1 course.

Reflection: Quran & Sunnah in Work Ethic

Islamic teachings inspire me to uphold values like sincerity (ikhlas), excellence (ihsan), and responsibility (amanah). These guide my actions as a student, worker, and member of society. Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said: "Verily, Allah loves when anyone of you does a job, he should perfect it." I try to reflect this by delivering quality, respecting deadlines, and serving others with intention. These values were echoed in my Crisis Management role and throughout USRAH 1 reflections.
